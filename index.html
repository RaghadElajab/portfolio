Crop Disease Classification

A PyTorch image-classification study that identifies 12 maize and cashew health conditions while benchmarking four CNN architectures.

95.73% best accuracyPythonPyTorchEfficientNet
  • Prepared and validated 11,838 crop images spanning five cashew classes and seven maize classes.
  • Created stratified training, validation, and test splits with augmentation, ImageNet normalization, and class-weighted loss.
  • Compared a custom CNN with transfer-learned ResNet-18, EfficientNet-B0, and VGG-16 models using early stopping and best-checkpoint restoration.
  • Evaluated class-level precision, recall, F1 scores, confusion matrices, ROC curves, and held-out test accuracy.
  • Reached 95.73% test accuracy on cashew and 85.89% on maize with EfficientNet-B0.
